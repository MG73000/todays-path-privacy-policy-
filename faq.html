Subscription FAQ

Last updated: July 24, 2025

How much does the app cost?

Today’s Path is free to try for 3 days. After that, you can subscribe for $2.99/month to unlock premium features.

What’s included in the subscription?

  • Daily AI-based reflections
  • Advanced journaling prompts
  • Motivational quotes & verse notifications
  • Streak tracking and personalized support

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open your iPhone Settings > Your Apple ID > Subscriptions. Select Today’s Path and tap “Cancel”.

Will I be charged after my trial?

Yes, unless you cancel at least 24 hours before the end of your 3-day trial.

Can I get a refund?

All payments are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund.
