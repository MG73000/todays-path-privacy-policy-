Subscription FAQ

Last updated: July 24, 2025

How much does the app cost?

Today’s Path is free to download and includes a 3-day free trial. After that, a subscription costs $2.99/month to continue using premium features.

What’s included in the subscription?

  • Daily AI-powered reflections based on your journal entries
  • Morning and evening journaling prompts tailored to your needs
  • Motivational quotes and Catholic Bible verse notifications
  • Streak tracking and personalized emotional support

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime via your iPhone:

  • Go to Settings
  • Tap your name > Subscriptions
  • Select Today’s Path and tap Cancel Subscription

Will I be charged after the free trial?

Yes. If you don’t cancel at least 24 hours before your trial ends, your subscription will begin automatically at $2.99/month through your Apple ID account.

Can I get a refund?

We do not process refunds directly. To request one, visit Apple’s refund portal at:
https://reportaproblem.apple.com

Can I still use the app without subscribing?

Yes! You can continue to use basic features of the app as a guest. However, subscription unlocks full journaling access, AI reflections, and streak tracking.

Need help?

We’re here for you. Please email us at support@todayspath.com and we’ll respond as soon as possible.
