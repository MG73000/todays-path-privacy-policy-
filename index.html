Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 24, 2025

Today’s Path is a journaling and spiritual reflection app designed to help users through difficult moments in life. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information.

What We Collect

  • Name, email address (if you sign in with Apple or Google)
  • Custom display name and selected area of struggle
  • Journal entries (encrypted and stored securely)
  • Device information (used for notifications)
  • Anonymous usage data for improving the app

How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize your experience and daily reflections
  • To generate AI responses using a secure OpenAI integration
  • To track your progress and send motivational notifications
  • To provide secure authentication and sync data via Firebase

How Your Data Is Protected

  • Journal entries are encrypted locally before upload
  • All data is transmitted securely using HTTPS
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to power Today’s Path:

  • Firebase – for authentication, database, and notifications
  • OpenAI – for AI-based journaling responses
  • Apple StoreKit – for subscription management

Your Rights

  • You may request deletion of your account and data at any time
  • You can opt out of notifications in the app settings
  • You have the right to access your data on request
